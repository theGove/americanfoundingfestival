Liberty Village Colonial Festival Event Details

General information about the event is here. This page will be updated from time to time. If you have additional questions or would like additions to this page, please text Holly at 760-670-8832

Event location

Washington County Fairgrounds located in Hurricane, Utah. Legacy Park 5500 West 700 South Hurricane, Utah 84737. Here is the link to the Washington County Fair, for your information. https://washcofair.net/You may be interested to know that Lee Greenwood will be in concert there Wednesday night (tickets required).

Setup Time

Set up on Wednesday the 16th. See map for individual locations.

Dates

Thursday, April 17th – Saturday the 19th, 2025 .

Hours

The Colonial Festival is open from 10am to 7pm, during the last three days of the annual County Fair, which draws thousands of people each day. Please be ready to greet the public by 10am Thursday – Saturday.

The fair official hours are 4:00 pm - 10:00 pm on Thursday and Friday, 10:00 am - 10:00 pm on Saturday. The Colonial festival will be open to invited 5th grade classes on Thursday beginning at 10:00 am, and open to the public beginning at 10:00 am on Friday.

Food

Lunch will be provided Thursday - Saturday but you are on your own for the other meals. The carnival starts Thursday night and there will be many food vendors from that time forward. They are not expected to be open for breakfast.

Holly is still reaching out to personally verify lunch numbers and dietary restrictions: Please save her contact information to help facilitate the contact: Holly White 760-670-8832

Lodging

As we arrange accommodation for your upcoming stay, we want to ensure we’re making the most of the resources available. Onsite camping: A good number of you will be camping onsite. The average temperature in Hurricane in April is a low of 40 to a high of 80 degrees, beautiful camping weather!  Period tents are necessary to camp in the area or there is RV parking with 30 amp hook ups (no water).

Restroom and Showers

There are many restroom available and two showers. See restroom map for locations.

Showers are located in the building just south of the RV parking. There is a key and a code necessary to obtain entrance. During festival hours, Holly, in the Colonial Relatives area (space 2 on the location map), will have the keys and will maintain shower schedule to make best use of the two shower stalls. (1 man/ 1 woman). We will coordinate with someone who is camping on site to hold the key after hours.

Host Homes

Many of you have agreed to stay in a host home and placement is currently being arranged. Holly will let you know in the next few days about your host home locations. We believe that staying with a host family will be an enriching experience for you and the family and we welcome feedback about your experience.

You will only be placed in a host home of you indicated that as a preference on your registration form. If you have not heard from Holly about the details of your host home, we were unable to place you with a family and you'll need to make other arrangements.

Lodging and Travel Reimbursement

We do have funds to help with lodging and travel expenses. These are taxpayer dollars, so we want to treat them judiciously and with respect. Please look at these reimbursement links to familiarize yourself with how you will submit receipts for travel and lodging, they are two separate forms.

Travel Reimbursement Form

Lodging Reimbursement Form

Please note that we have a cap of $100 per night per hotel room. This anticipates two participants will share a room. If you have a friend or fellow participant you’d like to room with, we encourage you to coordinate with them. If you have three people in a room $150 would be the reimbursement cap. It breaks down to $50/per person per night. Feel free to secure a short term rental for larger groups. Please explore other options with Holly before booking a room for a single individual if you are seeking reimbursement.
